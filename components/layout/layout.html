Workspace

Welcome to Lime.

Lime is your creative workspace — built on Seed, designed for artists, musicians, and builders who move fast and care about how things feel. Everything here is composable, intentional, and yours to shape.

Layout demo

Use the controls above to switch panels between docked and floating modes, collapse the sidebars, or drag the resize handles on panel edges. Everything transitions at 200ms ease — no layout jumps.

The left panel collapses to an icon strip — labels slide away, icons stay. The right panel hides completely. In floating mode, panels detach from the edge with rounded corners and a shadow, sitting as cards over the canvas background.

Resize handles live on the inner edges. Drag them to adjust panel widths between 180px and 400px. The handle turns lime when you grab it.
